Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is required for our beginner courses. We start from the fundamentals and gradually build your skills. For intermediate and advanced courses, we recommend having the prerequisite knowledge.

What is the class schedule like?

We offer flexible scheduling with both part-time (evenings and weekends) and full-time immersive options. Most courses include 2-3 live sessions per week plus self-paced assignments.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes! Our career services include resume reviews, interview preparation, portfolio development, and connections with our hiring partners. We're committed to helping you launch your tech career.

Can I pay in installments?

Absolutely. We offer various payment plans including monthly installments. We also have scholarships available for qualified applicants. Contact our admissions team for details.

What kind of projects will I build?

You'll build real-world projects ranging from personal websites to full-stack applications. By the end of your course, you'll have a professional portfolio showcasing your skills to potential employers.
