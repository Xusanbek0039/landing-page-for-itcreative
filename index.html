Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is needed for our beginner courses. We start from the absolute basics and gradually build your skills. For advanced courses, we recommend having foundational knowledge of programming concepts.

How much time should I commit each week?

We recommend dedicating 15-20 hours per week for optimal progress. This includes classroom time, practice sessions, and project work. However, you can adjust based on your personal schedule and learning pace.

Will I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes! All graduates receive an industry-recognized certification. More importantly, you'll have a portfolio of projects that demonstrate your skills to potential employers.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Absolutely. Our career services team helps with resume building, interview preparation, and connecting you with our network of hiring partners. We also host regular networking events with industry professionals.
